# Photography & Cinematography Math

Technical rules for capturing professional imagery.

## Exposure Triangle
- **ISO**: Double ISO = Double brightness. Stay lowest for minimum noise.
- **Aperture**: f/2.8 (shallow DOF) vs f/11 (sharp background).
- **Shutter**: 1/Focal Length minimum for handheld.

## Focus & Optics
- **Hyperfocal Distance**: Focus 1/3 into scene at f/8-11 for maximum sharpness.
- **Eye Focus**: Always target the nearest eye for portraits.

## Natural Light Math
- **Golden Hour**: 1h after sunrise / 1h before sunset.
- **Blue Hour**: 20-30 mins after sunset.
- **Window Light**: Subject faces window, not camera.

## RAW Workflow
- RAW recovers 2-3 stops of exposure.
- 3-2-1 Backup Rule: 3 copies, 2 media types, 1 offsite.
