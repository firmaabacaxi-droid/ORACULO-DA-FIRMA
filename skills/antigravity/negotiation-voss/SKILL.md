# Tactical Negotiation (Voss Style)

Negotiation is the act of discovery, not an argument.

## Core Techniques
- **Mirroring**: Repeat last 1-3 words with upward inflection.
- **Labeling**: "It seems like...", "It sounds like...". Name the emotion to defuse it.
- **Calibrated Questions**: "How am I supposed to do that?", "What's the biggest challenge?".
- **Accusation Audit**: List every negative thing they might think about you upfront.

## The Workflow
1. **Preparation**: Accusation audit + calibrated questions.
2. **Opening**: Tactical empathy. Defuse objections immediately.
3. **Closing**: Rule of Three (confirm 3 times). Ackman Method for pricing (65% -> 85% -> 95% -> 100%).

Target the "That's Right" response — it signals genuine agreement.
