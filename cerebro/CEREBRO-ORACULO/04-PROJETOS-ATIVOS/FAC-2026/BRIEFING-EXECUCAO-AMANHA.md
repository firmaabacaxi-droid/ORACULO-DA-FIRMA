### Prompt 1 — Sael: apresentação do personagem
```
A lone male circus performer, barefoot, wearing wide linen cream-white pants and an open linen shirt. A rope is tied around his waist as a belt. He stands in a neutral pose against a backdrop of white draped fabric with a soft blue-indigo light projection. The aesthetic is contemporary circus meets folk storytelling. Full body, theatrical, slightly exaggerated proportions suggesting commedia dell'arte influence. Cinematic lighting, film grain texture.
```

### Prompt 2 — A Cenografia: o palco vivo
```
An empty contemporary circus stage with large white fabric panels suspended from ropes and pulleys at different heights and angles, creating an architectural landscape of cloth. The fabrics are lit from behind with soft projection light. The space feels alive and transformable, like a blank canvas that can become any world. No performers, just the stage potential. Wide shot, theatrical photography style.
```

### Prompt 3 — Os Adereços: objetos de cena
```
A flat lay composition of circus performance props arranged on white fabric: an antique oil lantern, a delicate white veil with a flower tiara, a worn leather hat with a wooden pipe, a single feather, a dark shawl with thin golden threads, a jackknife. A rough rope coiled at the center. Theatrical still life, soft dramatic lighting, slight texture grain. Each object tells a story.
```

### Prompt 4 — Prólogo: O Lar
```
Video projection mapped onto suspended white fabric panels creating the interior of a humble wooden fisherman's cottage. Warm amber lantern light flickers on the fabric walls. Through a gap in the fabric, a dark sea with reflected stars. Slow moving ocean waves visible outside. Intimate, warm, like a memory of childhood. The projection technique is clearly visible — this is fabric, not a real room. Cinematic still, theatrical.
```

### Prompt 5 — Elara: a torre de vidro
```
Video projection mapped onto white fabric panels: soft pink and lavender flowers blooming across the fabric surfaces, petals in rose-pale (#F8C8D4) and lavender (#C8A8E4). In the upper fabric, constellations appear with thin cursive star names written in light. A European fairy tale atmosphere — delicate, ethereal. The fabric architecture suggests a glass tower. Cinematic, dreamlike, theatrical.
```

### Prompt 6 — Moacir: a floresta e a pedra
```
Video projection mapped onto white fabric panels: dense dark night forest, deep jungle greens and black, earth tones. A warrior's silhouette in the fabric center slowly turning to stone, becoming part of the landscape. In the far background projection, a distant village with firelight. Ochre, dark green, near black palette. Heavy, grounded, indigenous Brazilian aesthetic. Cinematic theatrical still.
```

### Prompt 7 — Ísola: os fios do destino
```
Video projection mapped onto white fabric panels: countless golden threads of light crossing the fabric in all directions, creating a visible fate map. A figure with a dark shawl moves hands through the projected threads. Deep purple (#4A0E4E), burgundy, shadow-gold palette. Romani mystical aesthetic. Theatrical, dramatic, intimate.
```

### Prompt 8 — A Crise: o colapso visual
```
Video projection mapped onto white fabric panels: fragments of all previous environments — cottage, flowers, jungle, threads, cosmos — overlapping and spinning simultaneously. The shadow of a single performer multiplied across all fabric surfaces. Visual chaos, all color palettes competing, fragmented and pulsing. High emotional intensity. Expressionist theatrical still.
```

### Prompt 9 — A Revelação: o branco absoluto
```
A single male performer sitting in stillness center stage. Behind him: pure white fabric, no projection at all. Minimal warm spotlight on his face and body. Empty, quiet, absolute. The entire set stripped back to white fabric and one human being. Cinematic, peaceful, profound. Contemporary circus theater aesthetic.
```
